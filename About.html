About Us

Welcome to Burger King, the home of the Whopper! Since our founding in 1954, we have been committed to serving high-quality, affordable food to our customers. We believe in providing delicious, flame-grilled burgers made with fresh ingredients. Our mission is to bring joy to people's lives through great food and exceptional service.

Our Story

Started in Miami, Florida, Burger King has grown into a global brand with over 18,000 locations in more than 100 countries. Our success is built on our passion for great food and our dedication to customer satisfaction. We constantly innovate and adapt to the changing tastes and preferences of our customers while staying true to our roots.

Our Values

At Burger King, we value quality, customer service, and community. We strive to use fresh, high-quality ingredients in all our products. Our commitment to our customers is unwavering, and we aim to create a welcoming and enjoyable dining experience for everyone. We also believe in giving back to the communities we serve through various initiatives and partnerships.

Sustainability

We are dedicated to sustainability and environmental responsibility. From sourcing our ingredients responsibly to reducing our carbon footprint, we take significant steps to ensure that our operations positively impact the planet. Our goal is to create a sustainable future for our customers and communities.

Join Our Team

At Burger King, we believe in providing opportunities for growth and development. We offer a supportive and dynamic work environment where you can thrive. Join our team and be a part of a global brand that values hard work, creativity, and innovation.
